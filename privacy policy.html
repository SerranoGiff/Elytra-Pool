Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data and digital rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide such as your name, email address, and wallet address. We also collect technical data including IP address, browser type, and blockchain activity.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver and operate our staking services.
  • To enhance security and detect fraudulent activity.
  • To comply with legal requirements.
  • To communicate updates or promotions (opt-out available).

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information with trusted service providers such as KYC processors, infrastructure partners, and legal authorities when required.

4. Data Retention

  • KYC records are stored for 5 years unless required longer by law.
  • Blockchain data is publicly available and retained indefinitely.
  • Cookies and session data may be retained for up to 30 days.

5. Security Measures

We use encryption, secured wallets, and regular audits to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure. Use of the platform is at your own risk.

6. Your Rights

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent or opt out of communications.
  • Exercise additional rights under applicable privacy laws.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of Elytra Pool indicates your acceptance of the revised terms.

8. Contact Us

If you have privacy concerns or questions, you can contact us:

  • Email: privacy@elytrapool.com
  • Website: https://elytrapool.com/contact
